Our Process

The Water Out® patented drying process cannot be duplicated. It is an unbelievable air exchange system using outside air and replacing the wet, moist and humid effected air this creates a warm dry Arizonia day inside your building. The Water Out trailers manufacture new fresh outside air and remove the interior wet air instantaneously. The drying system will dry the building materials and flooring quickly so as to eliminate any chance for mold and further water damage.

Water Out® of Northeast Florida uses fresh outside air as the drying source. The outside air is conditioned within the Water Out trailer and introduced into a flooded building at about 2%RH (relative humidity). The super-dry air is circulated through a flooded building and the wet contaminated air is exhausted to the outside of the building. This "Open Air Exchange" concept leaves a clean, dry building in only a few days. Standard dehumidifiers require a large amount of extra power to run their "mini" refrigerant systems and blowers throughout a wet building. They waste energy to convert wet air to water and pump it to a drain. This type of technology was designed before most of us were born. Water Out runs on very little power and does the work of several standard dehumidifiers. This leaves us the extra power to run the needed air movers to release the wet air and exhaust it from the building. Drying with fresh outside air seems very clear - Water Out wins hands down! Water Out is quiet too! Inaudible from more than twenty feet Water Out will not disturb neighbors. Water Out is the fastest most cost effective drying process in the world. Water Out equipment and trained, certified operators are in most US locations and growing both in the United States and abroad. When you need Water Out, we will be there.
